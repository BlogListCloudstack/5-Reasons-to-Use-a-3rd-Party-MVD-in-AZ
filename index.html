What Are 3rd Party Motor Vehicle Services?

An Authorized Third Party (ATP) office is run by a private company that is contracted by Arizona’s Motor Vehicle Division (MVD).

These companies offer all MVD services on behalf of the MVD and will typically be more convenient for drivers. Third-party MVD services tend to be less crowded, more efficient, and offer more extensive service hours.

In many cases, it’s a much better choice to visit a third-party MVD location instead of a typical MVD field office.
